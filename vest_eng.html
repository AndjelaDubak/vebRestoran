Absolutely the best way to make steak, tested several times

  • 10 min ago
  • 1 comment
  • PREP TIME
    2h
Ingredients
  • Dijon mustard in the amount of one tablespoon
  • a few cloves of garlic
  • a pinch of onion powder
  • soy sauce for sushi, you can add to taste, but you should pay attention - it is a concentrate or diluted
  • plant oil
  • a small amount of Worcestershire sauce
  • balsamic vinegar 20 ml
  • dried basil can be added more, up to three tablespoons
  • dried primrose to taste, but three tablespoons will suffice
  • hot sauce as desired and the amount is not limited
Directions
  • 1. The meat needs to be removed earlier in order to warm it to room temperature. Count on standing out of the fridge for at least an hour to achieve this.
  • 2. The second step in preparing steak is to determine the right thickness of the meat. It should never be thin, but it is recommended that the thickness of the steak be about 3 cm, weighing about 250 g / person.
  • 3. Making a marinade. It is desirable to stand in the marinade for 2 days, and maybe only half an hour. The longer it stands the better.
  • 4. Steak can be baked in the oven, grill or frying pan, and when baking, the most important thing is that the meat is at room temperature and that it is salted just before baking.
